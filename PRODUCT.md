# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Music fans, prospective ticket buyers, and festival partners exploring a Jakarta event, often on a mobile device after discovering it through social media. They need the festival identity, date, location, lineup, and ticket path to be immediately clear.

## Product Purpose

Nocturne Fest is a premium, frontend-only festival landing experience that sells the atmosphere and makes the event's programme and simulated ticket flow easy to explore.

## Brand Personality

Cinematic, kinetic, and nocturnal. The experience should feel like an immersive festival poster: high-energy and futuristic, but controlled enough that key information remains effortless to read.

## Anti-references

Avoid generic event templates, corporate dashboard patterns, random or excessive neon, plain shallow cards, and motion that slows or obscures content.

## Design Principles

- Make the festival name, date, place, and ticket action unmistakable in the first screen.
- Use lighting, imagery, and motion to create atmosphere without competing with information.
- Let every section have a deliberate visual role rather than repeating a generic card pattern.
- Preserve generous, intentional spacing across desktop and mobile.
- Keep simulated interactions clearly labelled and trustworthy.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Maintain readable contrast on dark surfaces, semantic and keyboard-operable controls, visible focus states, touch-friendly targets, and reduced-motion alternatives for visual effects.
